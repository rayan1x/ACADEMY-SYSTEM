F.A.Q

What is ACADEMY SYSTEM ?
ACADEMY SYSTEM is a platform for education management and training dedicated to educational centers, allowing you to manage students, teachers, classes, attendances, notes, billing and more, all online.
How to register for a training ?
Click on the "Explore the Academy" button or go to the Formations section, then follow the instructions to create your account and choose your pack.
What are the payment methods accepted ?
We accept payments by credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Amazon Pay and bank transfer.
Can I access my courses at any time ?
Yes, the platform is accessible 24h/24 and 7j/7 from any connected device to the Internet.
Are the certificates recognized ?
Yes, our certificates are recognized by our partner centers and attest to the follow-up and success of your training.
How to contact support ?
You can contact us via the Support section, by email at academiesystem@gmail.com or via the contact form on the site.
Can I change my pack later ?
Yes, it is possible to switch to a higher pack at any time from your member space.
Do you offer discounts for groups or companies ?
Yes, special offers are available for groups, schools or companies. Contact us for a personalized quote.
